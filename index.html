What we can Do

We provide our clients with a full range of veterinary services. Our doctors carefully observe the standards of civilized veterinary medicine, strictly follow the regulations and put the interests of the patient first in any situation. Our choice is modern evidence-based medicine based on scientific knowledge.

Veterinarian
The doctor will assess the condition of the pet, carry out the necessary diagnostics, identify the pathology, prescribe treatment and, if necessary, refer to a highly specialized specialist.
Dentistry
Animals also have dental problems. They appear due to improper or insufficient hygiene, unbalanced nutrition and the genetic characteristics of the pet.
Surgery
When medications fail, tests do not give an accurate result, or the pet is in critical condition, surgery can help him.
